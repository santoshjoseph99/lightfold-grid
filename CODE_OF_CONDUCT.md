# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Lightfold Grid a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Welcome different viewpoints and levels of experience.
- Give and accept technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's information.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or sexualized attention.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Knowingly exposing secrets, vulnerabilities, or unsafe instructions.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's private
security-reporting channel. Maintainers will review reports promptly, protect reporter
privacy where possible, and may remove content, restrict participation, or ban
participants.

This code applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the project.
Enforcement decisions may be appealed to a maintainer who was not involved in the
original decision when one is available.
